Special Arrabiata Sauce

This Spicy Arrabbiata Sauce recipe is super-simple to make, calls for just 9 main ingredients, and is guaranteed to kick your pasta nights up a delicious notch.

What you're going to need...

  • Tomatoes (3-4 medium ones) — the redder, the better!
  • 400g of crushed tomatoes
  • 50-75ml of tomato paste
  • 2 finely chopped (small) onions
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of sambal olek
  • For flavouring: paprika, pepper, salt, lemon juice (ideally wine vinegar)
  • Chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes

Let's Get Cooking!

  1. Chop up tomatoes into very small pieces.
  2. Heat up your skillet with olive oil.
  3. Add in the onions and let them cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add cut up tomatoes and cook for 1-2 minutes
  5. Add crushed tomatoes and garlic and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Stir periodically and use wooden spoon to break up the tomato pieces.
  7. Add your flavourings – sambal olek, paprika, salt, pepper, lemon juice.
  8. Add chopped basil and chilli flakes.
  9. Add water so that it doesn’t become too thick.
  10. Let it sit for a while, and keep stirring.
  11. Keep it over a simmer until ready to eat.
